Risotto - Delicious Italian Dish

Classic Risotto Recipe

Risotto is a creamy and comforting Italian dish made with rice, broth, butter, and Parmesan cheese. It's perfect for any occasion and can be customized with a variety of ingredients like mushrooms, seafood, or vegetables. Follow this recipe for a delicious and rich flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
